THIS IS ME

I am a game designer from with a background in computer science and film. I believe that game design is an art form of creating experiences for other people. I pride myself on learning everything possible and tapping life experiences in order to create virtual worlds for people to explore. I want to help bring video games into widespread acceptance as art and not just a form of entertainment. I believe that games can make people reflect, criticize and feel emotions the same way movies or music can. So let's make art together.

THESE ARE THINGS

Not Bomberman

Team created a network version of bomberman for our networked game project course. Game was built in the Unity engine for client side and used JSON/ javascript for the server side.

Jasper Samurai

A 2D action platformer game created for the Video Game Development club. Used Unity game engine and c# for development. The player takes on the role of the blue hornet samurai and must save the world tree from evil. We originally designed three levels for the game but due to a team downsize of 50% only one level was fully finished.

Wizard Run

A 2D endless runner game where the player must avoid obstacles and collect gems and survive. Gems provide power-ups to the player to help them. Game was created during the 2016 Global Game Jam. Team consisted of three people, 2 programmers 1 artist.
