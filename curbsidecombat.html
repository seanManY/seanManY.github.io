Curbside Combat

A 2D fighting game creating for the Senior Capstone class. The project was created by a team of 4 people and over the course of 20 weeks. The idea of this fighting game was to help players familiarize themselves with fighting games. The goal was to make something that was two steps below a traditional fighting . For example only having 4 button commands (Heavy, medium, light, special).

I worked on a frame data tool to help the workflow. A script that parsed frame values from a google doc into the game engine. In addition to that I also was responsible for the sound design and implementation for the game as well as helping with miscellaneous tasks and bugs.

Special thanks to our character artist who did every frame of our character, without her the game really wouldn't exist.
