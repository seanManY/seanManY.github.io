About

TL; DR

I am a game designer who considers himself an artist who just loves everything that games has to offer.

An actual bio

Ever since I was 6 I knew I wanted to make games. I started programming in middle school and made text based versions of games such as hangman and battleship. I went on to learn actionscript and made some janky flash games then GameMaker and eventually onto Unity. Majority of my experience is from school projects and game jams. I try to go to as many game jams as I can to help my creative need to produce work. I definitely gravitate towards the design of games and wonder what a game can do for society. I question why games matter and who we are as gamers. Since graduation I have focused my efforts on creating small “art games” as a form of creative expression of my life.
