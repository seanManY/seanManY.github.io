Iterations

  • Low Fidelity Wireframes
    For our new designs, we wanted to update the navigation and make the hero image more modern and impactful. We also wanted to update the content chucking to make information easier to read and digestible.
  • Mid Fidelity Wireframes
    After consolidating our ideas and sketches we decided the new website needed more visuals such as pictures and infographics to convey digestible information.
  • High Fidelity Designs
    After conducting some usability tests we found that our original prototype still seemed dense with text. So we added more visuals and integrated typography to show data and statistics.
