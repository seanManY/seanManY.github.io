Problem Statement

Our non-profit website was designed to inform users about the urgency of aiding children in detention centers and provide an opportunity for involvement. We have observed that our website’s homepage hierarchy doesn’t highlight a sense of urgency, which is causing users to be wary of making monetary donations. How might we improve transparency so that our users are successful based on an uptick in donations and an increased trust in the organization?​​​​​​​
