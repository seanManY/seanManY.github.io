Blue Hornet Samurai

One of the most game-like games I have had the privilege of working on. 2D action platformer made by a team of 6, that slowly went down to 3, over a course of an academic school year. You play as a samurai, who is also a blue hornet, on a mission to hunt down the hummingbird queen, unfortunately the hummingbird queen boss had to be cut. The character has multiple abilities such as a attack, guard, projectiles, super jump and dash.

I worked on designing the enemies and bosses for the game. Most of which were cut due to our team shrinking due to school and life getting in the way and time constraints.
