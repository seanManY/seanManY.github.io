Exciting Title

A 2D puzzle platformer for the 2018 UCI Hackathon. This game was made by a team of 3 over the course of 36 hours. The game has two separate levels. The Brawn level which tests the players platforming abilities and the Brain level where it tests the players puzzle skills. The player is giving the option to choose which levels to play depending on their own play style. The game also features many character mechanics and items such as double jump, dash, locked doors and a triple jump item.

Unfortunately given the stress and time limitations of the hackathon a lot of level ideas and concepts had to be tabled. There were also difficulties with the git repo where at the last minute, reverted to an older version of the game, deleting half of our levels. What remains are the scraps of levels we could find. We intend to continue working on this project soon.
