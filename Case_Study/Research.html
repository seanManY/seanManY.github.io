Research

Affinity Diagram

From our interviews and our survey, we discovered that prospective donors lack assurance that donations are being used properly and want to see success stories which derive from donations. We believe we might be able to help if we emphasize success stories and a breakdown of how donations are utilized for the cause.

Empathy Map

After consolidating our user research we created a new User Persona and Empathy Map identifying our user’s needs and wants.
