Project Overview

A three-week case study where I and three other UX designers, had to redesign a website for a non-profit organization. The non-profit we chose was Every Last One, an organization “dedicated to expediting the release of all immigrant children from the trauma of government detention. [They] help to reunite them with families when possible or with carefully screened, loving community-based sponsors when not.”
